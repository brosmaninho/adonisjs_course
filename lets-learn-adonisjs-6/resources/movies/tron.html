Tron: Legacy

Tron: Legacy is a 2010 American science fiction action film directed by Joseph Kosinski. It is a sequel to the 1982 film Tron and stars Garrett Hedlund, Olivia Wilde, Jeff Bridges, and Bruce Boxleitner.

The film follows Sam Flynn, the son of Kevin Flynn, who is pulled into the digital world of Tron where he must rescue his father and stop a rogue program from taking over the real world.

The film features stunning visual effects and a groundbreaking soundtrack by Daft Punk, which received critical acclaim.

Tron: Legacy was released in 3D and IMAX, and it was praised for its visual style and cinematography, although some critics felt the plot was lacking.
